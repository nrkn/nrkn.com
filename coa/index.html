Happy 1st Birthday Cats of Auckland!

Cats of Auckland is an organisation dedicated to sharing our values. Values such as cat stalking, cat photography and the aim of creating the world's first cat paparazzi. We are proud to celebrate our very first birthday with you, our valued members. Take the day off, you've earned it. And if you've got a spare minute, put your feet up and take a look over some of the highlights of our first year.

What is Cats of Auckland?

Cats of Auckland is a Facebook group for sharing pictures and stories about the random cats people befriend on the mean streets of Auckland, New Zealand. Imagine Humans of New York. But cats. In Auckland. Partly inspired by the Wellington zine “Cat Neighbours”.
