Windows vs Mac

We present the facts - you decide!

Windows

Pros

  • Lets in natural light
  • Improves air circulation
  • Provides egress for pet cats

Cons

  • Chance of bird strike
  • Common entry point for burglars
  • Inedible

Mac

Pros

  • Cheap, easy to prepare
  • Tasty comfort food
  • Can be dressed up with supplementary ingredients

Cons

  • High in calories and sodium
  • Unsuitable for people with gluten or lactose intolerance
  • Not structurally sound when part of a wall
