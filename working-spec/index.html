communicate complex building details through interactive animated 3D models

Working Spec creates interactive animated 3D models of your product assembly or application instructions and puts them onto users’ mobile devices — making it faster and easier for your products to be assembled or applied correctly.

Find Out More

So how does it work?

1

Working Spec creates a 3D model of assembly or application instructions from manufacturers and architects

2

QR code is applied to product brochures, packaging and 2D building plans

3

The user downloads Working Spec app then scans the QR code

4

The user interacts with 3D model to quickly and easily understand how components fit together

5

Product is assembled or applied correctly, saving time and money, making the manufacturer, builder, architect and home owner very happy.

Save Time and Money

Working Spec can quickly create cost-effective 3D models from your existing 2D assembly and application instructions, saving you the expense of making in-house videos.

Existing Platform

No need to develop or maintain your own app at great expense. All you need is to add your content to an existing platform which is constantly being updated and improved on.

Easy to Understand

Interactive animated 3D models are much easier to understand than 2D drawings, and much less time consuming than watching videos showing assembly in action. Viewers with English as a second language can also understand 3D animations.

Easy to Use

QR codes on brochures, product packaging and building plans make it easy for a person to access 3D models on their mobile phones. No technical skills or special software is required to download 3D models.

Improve Building Quality

Communicating assembly and application instructions clearly through interactive animated 3D models will reduce construction mistakes, resulting in a better quality build. And when your building components are correctly assembled, they’ll function properly.

Speed up Construction

Interactive animated 3D models mean builders will spend less time puzzling over complex 2D drawings, speeding up the overall build process.

Assist your Sales Team

Working Spec 3D animations provide an engaging way for Product and sales representatives to explain complex assembly or application instructions. When specifiers and builders understand your product, they’re more likely to recommend it for a project.
